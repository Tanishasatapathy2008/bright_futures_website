Divya

Divya's voice was a quiet thing at the start. During a poetry circle she murmured one line and blushed when the group applauded. Encouraged, she practiced until recitation felt natural. Her family noticed the change and began to celebrate her voice.

She now helps run reading circles, drawing quieter children into space and making them feel seen.
