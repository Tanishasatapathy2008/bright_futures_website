Tia

Tia loved drawing and resisted writing. A mentor suggested she write one sentence about each sketch. Sentences turned into paragraphs and then short stories. At a small reading, she shared a story about a parrot that could speak—and the room applauded.

Tia now creates little zines combining art and text, using creativity as a bridge to literacy.
