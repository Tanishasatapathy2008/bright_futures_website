Mrs. Das

Retired but restless, Mrs. Das wandered into a park class one Sunday and rediscovered teaching’s heartbeat. The children’s questions, their laughter, the slow clicks of understanding—she felt alive again. She began returning each weekend and quickly became beloved.

Her presence inspired other retirees to volunteer, bringing experience and warmth to our classes.
