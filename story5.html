Swati

Swati called numbers 'rakshas'—they scared her. Mrs. Das turned math into games with coins and laddoos. Swati started participating and even won multiplication races. Her confidence grew as her fear shrank.

Her grades improved and, more importantly, she found joy in learning. She now helps classmates who once outpaced her.
