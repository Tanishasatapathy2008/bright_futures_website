Your support keeps our weekend classrooms running. Here are practical ways to help:

  • Donate Stationery: Notebooks, pens, art supplies, and resource kits.
  • Monetary Support: Contribute to purchase kits, print materials, or sponsor a term.
  • Partnership: Partner with local schools, community groups, or businesses to scale the model.
