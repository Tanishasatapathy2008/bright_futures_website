Ms. Shraddha S. Tiwari

Ms. Shraddha used songs and playful rhymes to teach language. Harsh sounds and tricky letters became melodies, and children who once avoided speaking began to join in. Her warmth made the park feel like home for many learners.

Her methods created a space where learning was joyful—where mistakes were part of the fun and growth happened naturally.
