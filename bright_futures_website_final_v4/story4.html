Riya

Riya faced family resistance: extra schooling for girls was not a given in her home. She kept improving quietly and told her father, "Main padh kar doosri ladkiyon ko bhi padhana chahti hoon." Slow, steady progress softened his stance, and he bought her new notebooks.

Riya now tutors neighborhood girls and acts as a quiet leader; her story shows how a single determined student can reshape community expectations.
