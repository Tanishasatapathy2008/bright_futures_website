Ms. Anita Singh

Ms. Anita teaches with quiet authority. When a child angrily threw a pencil at a tricky fraction, she picked it up, smiled, and said, 'Fractions bhi thode patient hote hain.' The room laughed and tried again. Her gentle humor melted anxiety into curiosity.

Parents noticed new habits—persistence, attention, and steadier grades—grown from Anita Didi's calm consistency.
