Ansh

Ansh began at the last bench, convinced he wasn't capable. Ms. Anita Singh gave him one small task each week—a single problem on the board. The first attempts were shaky, but the community cheered every try.

Months later Ansh sprinted into class and shouted, "Didi, main top 10 mein hoon!" The celebration was for courage as much as it was for marks.
