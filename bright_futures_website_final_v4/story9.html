Mr. Ashok

Mr. Ashok taught life skills alongside academics. Once a boy was always late due to a punctured cycle; Mr. Ashok brought a small repair kit and taught him how to patch a tube. He said, 'Ab tum khud apna raasta taiyar kar sakte ho.' Punctures were fixed, and punctuality improved.

His lessons connect school with life—math for measuring, science for cooking, and repairs that build independence.
